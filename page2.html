About us

Specialists in building vibacrete walls.

Paarlberg Betonmure has been around for more than 30 years. We build beautiful fences for homes across the Winelands, Western Cape.

As an established contractor in the Paarl, Wellington, Pniel, Stellenbosch, Porterville, Tulbagh, Wolseley, Piketberg, Moorreesburg, Malmesbury areas, I can assure that I personally oversee every project and ensure that it is completed to high standards.

I have a passion for the construction industry and focus on quality and efficiency in all my projects. I am proud to say that I have a good reputation in the communities where I provide service and have a long list of satisfied clients.

I supply the material only or material with labour/installation. I have a dedicated team who are always willing to go the extra mile to ensure the best results.

Areas of service:
Paarl, Wellington, Pniel, Stellenbosch, Porterville, Tulbagh, Wolseley, Piketberg, Moorreesburg, Malmesbury

Feel free to contact me for a free quote - I will make sure that your project is completed as quickly and efficiently as possible.
